Food Safety

At Nene's Kitchen we ensure that your meat pie will always be safe to eat.

Your meat pie is being delivered either frozen or warm and ready to be eaten

If you order Frozen pie

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degree Fahrenheit.
  2. Re-heat pie for 35min.If using convection oven, 25 min
  3. If toaster oven is used ,please cover top of pie(s) with a piece of aluminium foil.
